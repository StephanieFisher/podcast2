Podcast Review 2

S-town

Structure

This podcast is structured like a murder mystery mixed with a radio story.

Who does the talking?

The reporter does most of the talking, but there are many interviews with the townsfolk as well.

Is it scripted?

Yes, insofar as the show was written and acted out by voice actors.

Interviews?

This show was basically based around interviews, with a good amount of foley mixed in.

Are there sound effects and music?

There are many sound effects during the interviews that give the impression that they were recorded on location, ie; gravel crunching underfoot; the sharpening of a chainsaw; wind whipping around, ect, ect.

Serial

Structure

Much like S-town (as it seems to be by the same people), this podcast is set up as a murder mystery investigation drama.

Who is talking?

The narrator does the vast majority of the speaking, with voice clips from some of the characters chiming in when appropriate.

Is it scripted?

Yes, but like the previous podcast, it is set up like a real investigation.

Interviews?

There are only voice clips from the interviewed, no questions are being asked in the clips themselves.

Are there sound effects and music?

There is some music to set the mood.
